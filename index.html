BROWN NOTE MUSIC (The Label)

Brown Note Music is the official record label founded by Karan Sky. It serves as the business backbone for releasing high-quality, authentic Indian rap music to a global audience.

We are committed to curating a sound that respects classic hip-hop foundations while pushing boundaries with international production and lyrical depth.

HARYANA CYPHER (The Movement)

Before the worldwide stages, there is the **Haryana Cypher**—a powerful movement built by Karan Sky to elevate and unify the rawest rap talent in the region.

This collective is the source of the hustle and the root of the rhythm, established as a showcase and a commitment to authentic storytelling and lyrical excellence, fostering the next generation of Indian hip-hop royalty.
